Ingredients:

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Summer Sausage
  • Jalapeño Cheese Spread
  • Pickle
  • Doritos
  • Cheetos
  • Mayo
  • water

Equipment:

  • Stinger or Microwave
  • Empty Family Size Chip Bag
  • Can lid or DOC ID Card

Directions:

  1. Crush the ramen noodles, doritos, and cheetos until they are in small chunks
  2. Dice the summer sausage and pickle
  3. Heat the water until just before boiling using the stinger or with a microwave
  4. Pour the noodles and chips into the empty chip bag. Then slowly add the water.
  5. Wrap the end of the bag, making sure to push out all of the air
  6. Let sit for 5 minutes, then add the summer sausage and pickle and mix.
  7. Cut the bag open and top with the mayo and the Jalapeño cheese
